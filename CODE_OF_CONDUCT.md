# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and kindness
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Respecting different viewpoints
- Taking responsibility and learning from mistakes

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may take corrective action for unacceptable behavior.
